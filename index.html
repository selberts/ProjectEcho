OUR MISSION

The Intramural Sports Program at Northwestern University strives to offer its students, faculty, and staff opportunities to recreate in an enjoyable way.

THE PROGRAM

IMs provide a chance for participation in sports in an organized, competitive environment White, Co-Rec and Purple leagues, depending on your level of skill!

TOURNAMENTS

Sports vary by quarter: dodgeball, volleyball and football in the fall, basketball and hockey in the winter, and soccer, softball and ultimate frisbee in the spring.
